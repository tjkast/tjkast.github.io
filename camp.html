FAQ

Do I have to attend all days of the camp?
No! In the registration link, you wil see that you can choose the days that your child will participate in.

Do I have to pickup materials?
Nope! If you have all the materials for the experiments that day, you do not need to pickup materials. However, we do package all the materials together for each experiment, so it might be more convinent (in terms of measuring) to pick-up at one of our locations all over Northern Virginia! After registration, yoi will get an email about picking up at your specified location.

Why is there a fee for the camp?
You only have to pay the fee if you are picking up materials, which is $3 per day's worth of materials.
